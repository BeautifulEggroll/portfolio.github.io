Throughout this journey, I've grown tremendously in confidence, technical skills, and leadership. Mentoring robotics students and helping organize events has given me the opportunity to develop my communication and teaching abilities. My favorite moment was when our robotics team won the Inspire Award and advanced through ILT—seeing our hard work recognized on such a scale was unforgettable.

Outside of team efforts, I also enjoyed teaching two younger siblings how to code Java using Minecraft, watching their eyes light up as they built their first mods. Looking ahead, I want to continue mentoring others, explore more advanced electronics and programming, and keep pushing my creative boundaries in STEM and beyond.
